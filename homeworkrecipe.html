How to cook Vegetarian Lentil Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dried lentils
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

1. Rinse the lentils under cold water and drain them.
2. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onions, carrots, and celery, and saut√© until softened, about 5 minutes.
3. Add garlic, cumin, coriander, paprika, salt, and pepper. Cook for another minute until fragrant.
4. Stir in tomato paste and cook for 1-2 minutes.
5. Add lentils, vegetable broth, bay leaf, and diced tomatoes to the pot. Bring to a boil.
6. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 20-25 minutes, or until lentils are tender.
7. Once lentils are cooked, taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
8. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley if desired. Enjoy your hearty vegetarian lentil soup!
